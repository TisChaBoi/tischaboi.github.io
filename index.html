Custom Computer Builds and Upgrades

I can help put together custom desktop PCs that can either blast its' way through even the most demanding games, or render 4k footage in Premiere Pro within minutes! Custom computers, as opposed to prebuilt computers, have the benifit of not having to pay for any brand logo, just the equipment itself, which means they're typically much cheaper. Also, these computers have no proprietary hardware, which means they are easily upgradable in the future, and will last you as long as you let it.

The build process for PCs typically takes around a month.

Device Diagnostics

I'll investigate any issues that you have with a device, and give you a checklist of anything I find. You can decide if you'd like me to do the repairs, or go another direction.

A typical diagnostic can usually be completed within an hour or two.

Device Repair

I will attempt to repair every device to the best of my ability, after a diagnosis or using provided parts. I will take special care to make sure your device returns to you in the best condition possible.

I also sell computers that I've repaired on my ​eBay page. Be sure to check it out if you're looking to buy a decently-priced restored PC!

Depending on the device, repair time can take anywhere from one to three weeks.

Drive Recovery/Destruction

I will use specialized hardware and software to attempt to revive data from a dead hard drive or SSD. To be completely honest, my techniques are nowhere near the level of skill used at large data recovery centers such as Data Savers, but for this reason, you're not paying steep data recovery center prices.

As for drive destruction, I use a specialized technique that I perfected at my last occupation to make sure any drive is completely destroyed and unreadable. This not only involves thoroughly wiping any data from the drive, but also completely dismantling the drive and shredding any components that might contain data.

Data recovery and drive destruction can take about two weeks.

Software Installation

I can help install any software, Windows updates, or drivers that may be causing issues making them hard to install. This usually takes less than a few days and can typically be resolved within an hour or two.

Repair Rates

Standard Repair
Each purchased replacement part will receive a 15% markup fee. I always strive to get the cheapest deal on any part, and by default, the parts condition will be used, unless otherwise requested or if a new part is cheaper. If a repair is done without a diagnostic, a $50 fee will be applied. Software repairs will have a flat fee of $50.

Warranty Repair
Any part covered by a warranty replacement can be swapped out for $10. If the claim is submitted by the customer, this fee can be waived.

Provided Part Repair
If the customer provides parts for installation and/or replacement, a flat fee of $100 will be charged. If a device requires a provided replacement and a diagnostic, this fee can be waived and will be rated as a diagnostic.

Custom Consultation/Build Rates

Full Spec & Build 
A full spec & build is considered when we put together a parts list from scratch based off a provided budget, the parts are ordered, and I put them all together. You will be consulted every step of the way while the list is being made. A full spec & build costs $175 in addition to the cost of parts, or 15% of the entire build. After a part list is finalized, 30% of the parts cost is required upfront before anything is ordered.

Spec Consultation
A spec consultation is when we put together a parts list from scratch based off a provided budget, with the intention of you buying the parts and building the PC yourself. You will be consulted every step of the way while building this list. A spec consultation costs $100. Additionally, if you already have a parts list you would like to have looked over and given suggestions, that can be done for $35.

Full Build
A full build is when a parts list is provided for me to build. A full build costs $75 in addition to the cost off parts, or 10% of the entire build. 30% of the parts cost is required upfront before anything is ordered.
