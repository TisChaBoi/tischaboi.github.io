Repair Rates

Standard Repair
Each purchased replacement part will receive a 15% markup fee. I always strive to get the cheapest deal on any part, and by default, the parts condition will be used, unless otherwise requested or if a new part is cheaper. If a repair is done without a diagnostic, a $50 fee will be applied.

Warranty Repair
Any part covered by a warranty replacement can be swapped out for $10. If the claim is submitted by the customer, this fee can be waived.

Provided Part Repair
If the customer provides parts for installation and/or replacement, a flat fee of $100 will be charged. If a device requires a provided replacement and a diagnostic, this fee can be waived and will be rated as a diagnostic.

Custom Consultation/Build Rates

Full Spec & Build 
A full spec & build is considered when we put together a parts list from scratch based off a provided budget, the parts are ordered, and I put them all together. You will be consulted every step of the way while the list is being made. A full spec & build cost $175 in addition to the cost of parts, or 15% of the entire build, whichever is the highest. After a part list is finalized, 30% of the parts cost is required upfront before anything is ordered.

Spec Consultation
A spec consultation is when we put together a parts list from scratch based off a provided budget, with the intention of you buying the parts and building the PC yourself. You will be consulted every step of the way while building this list. A spec consultation costs $75. Additionally, if you already have a prts list you would like to have looked over and given suggestions, that can be done for $35.

Full Build
A full build is when a parts list is provided for me to build. A full build costs $100 in addition to the cost off parts, or 10% of the entire build, whichever is higher. 30% of the parts cost is required upfront before anything is ordered.
